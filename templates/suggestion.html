Supercharge Your Resume & Job Hunt

Curated advice, templates, and strategies to make your resume stand out and land interviews faster.

💡 Resume Writing Tips

  • Use a clean, professional layout with readable fonts and clear headings.
  • Stick to 1 page (fresher) or 2 pages (experienced) — be concise.
  • Use strong action verbs and quantify achievements (e.g., “Reduced turnaround time by 30%”).
  • Customize your resume for each role using relevant keywords from the job description.
  • Proofread carefully — grammar and consistency matter.

📄 Recommended Resume Templates

  • Canva – Modern, visual templates perfect for creative roles.
  • Novoresume – Easy-to-edit ATS-friendly formats.
  • Zety – Templates with expert guidance and content suggestions.
  • Overleaf (LaTeX) – Ideal for developers and academic resumes.

🧠 Top Skills That Get You Hired

  • Technical: Python, JavaScript, SQL, Web Dev, Data Analytics, Cloud Platforms
  • Tools: Git, Docker, Figma, Power BI, VS Code, Excel
  • Soft Skills: Communication, Problem Solving, Adaptability, Teamwork
  • Certifications: AWS Cloud Practitioner, Google Data Analytics, Coursera/LinkedIn Skill Badges

🚀 Job Search Tips

  • Update LinkedIn regularly and include keywords in your headline/summary.
  • Get referrals by networking through alumni or meetups.
  • Track applications and follow up after interviews.
  • Use platforms like Internshala, AngelList, LinkedIn, Wellfound.
  • Build a portfolio or personal website to showcase your work.

📌 Final Suggestion from AI

"Your resume should tell your story — clearly, confidently, and tailored to your future."
